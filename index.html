Welcome!

I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the Vanderbilt Genetics Institute and the Department of Biomedical Informatics at Vanderbilt University Medical Center. Originally trained in chemistry and molecular biology, I have also completed a Masters in biomedical informatics on my path towards the PhD.

My work broadly focuses on harnessing the power of electronic medical records for precision medicine. From phenomics to pharmacogenomics, my primary goal is to help improve patient care by harnessing data, big and small alike. I am also passionate about health policy and its role in enabling innovation for patients and healthcare.

If you are interested in data science and R, you can also check out the materials for tutorials I've given in that domain. Otherwise, feel free to email me, follow me on Twitter (@GenomeGal), or download my CV for more details.
